What We Do

Founded in 2012, NovaBioAssays LLC is a Greater Boston-based contract research organization (CRO) specialized in providing innovative analytical and bioanalytical solutions to facilitate drug discovery and development. NovaBioAssays LLC occupies the two-story building with 12,000 SF space, and 10,000 SF for future expansion. We offer a full suite of analytical and bioanalytical services for quantitative and qualitative analysis of small molecules, oligonucleotides, peptides, and proteins. Our featured services include:

Bioanalysis
  • Small Molecule Bioanalysis
  • Peptide and Protein Quantification
  • Oligonucleotide Quantification and Metabolite Profiling
  • Biomarker Analysis

Protein Analytics
  • Intact Mass
  • Peptide Mass Mapping
  • Glycan Profiling and Glycosylation Site Identification
  • PTM Analysis
  • Sequence Variant Analysis
  • Antibody De Novo Sequencing
  • Antibody-Drug-Conjugate Characterization
  • Native MS (SEC-MS and IEX-MS)
  • cIEF-MS for Charge Variant
  • Separation Modes: SEC-UV, IEX-UV, HIC-UV, cIEF-UV, and RPLC-UV
  • Semi-Preparative Isolation (SEC, HIC, IEX, cIEF, RPLC)

Hydrogen Deuterium Exchange Mass Spectrometry (HDX MS)
  • Epitope/paratope mapping (antigen and antibody interaction characterization)
  • Protein-protein interaction characterization
  • Peptide/small molecule-protein interaction characterization
  • Peptide-oligonucleotide interaction characterization
  • Biosimilarity characterization

ADME
  • Drug Metabolite Identification and Profiling
  • Whole blood or plasma stability test
  • Microsomal and hepatocyte stability evaluation
  • Metabolite identification/profiling
  • Enzymatic reaction phenotyping
  • Protein binding evaluation using ultracentrifugation or RED (rapid equilibrium dialysis)
